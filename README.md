# Telecom Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard 📊

An interactive analytics dashboard built to analyze, visualize, and reduce *customer churn* in the telecommunications industry.

This project helps telecom companies gain actionable insights into customer behavior, identify high-risk customers, and implement effective retention strategies to minimize revenue loss.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry. This dashboard provides a clear view of churn patterns through key performance indicators and insightful visualizations.

### Key Metrics
- *Total Customers*: 20
- *Churned Customers*: 10
- *Churn Rate*: 50%
- *Total Revenue*: $40,054
- *High Risk Customers*: 8

---

## 📊 Visualizations & Insights

### Churn by Contract Type
Monthly contract customers show the *highest churn rate*, indicating weak long-term retention.

### Revenue Lost by State
- *Florida* has the highest revenue loss due to churn.
- Other affected states include Illinois, California, and Ohio.

### Support Calls vs Churn
Customers with a higher number of support calls are *significantly more likely* to churn.

### Satisfaction Score vs Churn
Low satisfaction scores strongly correlate with customer churn. Customers with satisfaction scores of 2 and 3 show the highest churn risk.

---

## 💡 Key Findings

- Monthly contract users are at the highest risk of leaving.
- Frequent support calls serve as an early warning signal for potential churn.
- Customer satisfaction is a major driver of retention — lower scores lead to higher churn.

## ✅ Recommendations

- Introduce *loyalty incentives* and better upgrade options for monthly contract customers to improve retention.
- *Improve customer support response time* to reduce churn risk from frustrated users.
- Proactively monitor and engage customers with *low satisfaction scores*.
